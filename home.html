Welcome to Profit Pharm

What a year it’s been! Best laid plans at the start of the year, which by mid-March were on the back-burner as COVID and it’s subsequent knock on effect on business took hold. It’s been really tough for community pharmacy and particularly so for independents. Many felt that their business was almost penalised for having to stay open especially with no footfall, front of shop sales plummeted across the sector. I’ve been amazed at the resilience shown by all those that I’ve spoken to over the recent months – and how for many enduring lockdown – their pharmacy was a valuable source of support.

As the year draws to a close – it’s time to focus on 2021. Hopefully as the threat from COVID subsides, the New Year will present an opportunity to look to the future, recover lost ground and thrive once more. At Profit Pharm, we also have had to adapt. Our normal work practices involve meeting clients face to face, delivering training and seminars in hotels etc, all of which came to an abrupt halt. We were quick adopters of online technology to keep going which has worked out reasonably well as a stop gap.

For 2021, we will be changing what we do and how we do it to a greater degree…. We will have news of that at our “Building Success 2021” online event. You are cordially invited to join us – it will be a great time to focus and to get ideas on how you can make next year a huge year for your business. Just click the button below to register. We will have a special guest also that evening, an independent community pharmacist how has rocketed from 212th in the country to now into the top 75. He will able to share some of his story and offer inspiration to us all.

It would be great if you could join us – if you have any questions or need further information, don’t hesitate to contact us – details throughout the site. Look forward to seeing you then,
Richard Kelly, Director at Profit Pharm
